What is Chlorpyrifos?

Chlorpyrifos or C9H11Cl3NO3PS, is chemical can be found primarily in pesticides. It has a white crystal appearance and is used primarily on farms. (Board, The Editorial.)

How does Chlorpyrifos get into the water?

Many pesticides contain chlorpyrifos. And when it rains after the pesticides have been used, then can the run off in streams into local watersheds. (“Chlorpyrifos.”)

What can you do to help wih Chlorpyrifos?

The best way to prevent chlorpyrifos from entering the enviroment is to discontinue the use of any pesticide and substances that could potenitally have bad chemicals in it.(EPA, Environmental Protection Agency)
