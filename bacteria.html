What diseases commonly found in water?

Many types of diseases can find its way into common drinking water. Some examples of diseases:

  • Salmonella
  • Cryptosporidium
  • E coli
  • Rotavirus
  • norovirus

All of these diseases have similar symptoms of nausea and sickness. And all of them get into the enviroment by the same way. (“Germs That Can Contaminate Tap Water.”)

How do diseases get into the water?

Most bacterias and viruses are able to make their way into watersheds from sewage system overflows and faults. (“Water Contamination and Diseases.”)

What can you do to stop the spread of Virus and Bacteria through water?

Start advocating for better standards of water, and quality of water systems. Most disease spread into the water by faults in sewage systems. By advocating for better water and sewage systems, you advocate for the probabiliy of sewage leaks to occur. Make sure you know who your water provider is and the water standards they use.

When you collect water from natural sources. If the water comes from outside, then make sure to boil the water before use, to clean the water and prevent yourself from being infected. (“Water Contamination and Diseases.”)
