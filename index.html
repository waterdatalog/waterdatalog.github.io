What is this website

WaterData is a website dedicated to sharing information about the types of pollution found within water. We show you what is in the water. How it gets into the water. And what you can do to prevent the spread of pollution.

Our Mission

The purpose of this website is meant to spread awareness of what is in the water that you drink. By cataloging what is in the water. You, yourself are able inform yourself about your drinking water.

Our Ending Goal

This website won't be the thing that stops water pollution. But, in order to achive that goal, everyone needs to contribute. And hopefully you'll find this website to be a helpful resourse in your research. Or maybe the start of your pollution activism journey.
